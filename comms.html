Comms response time

When a customer messages us by SMS or email, how long do they wait for our next message back on the same channel? Each point is the weekly mean response time — daily noise from evenings and weekends gets folded into one number per week (Monday-anchored). Only outbound messages from our CRM (human agent) or Robot Responder / AI Responder count as a reply — everything else (Message Factory, UIVR, Apply Website, Whitebox, App, Dialler, internal monitors) is ignored and the search jumps past it. Replies more than 14 days later are treated as "no reply within 14 days".

Data maturity: a message received in the last 14 days hasn't had its full chance to be replied to yet, so the chart only shows weeks where every message in them is 14+ days old. The current week and the last two are hidden.

The four lines split by the sender's state at the moment they sent the message: no ARef yet ("unknown"), have an ARef but no live loan ("applicant"), live loan and not in arrears ("live loan"), or live loan in arrears.

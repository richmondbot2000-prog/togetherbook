Top Ups

A top-up is a brand-new loan that pays off the customer's existing live loan and gives them more cash on top. A live loan becomes top-up eligible (TueStatus = true) when its balance, arrears history, and age all clear the lender's TUE thresholds.

Scope: US loans under the Transform Credit (LenderId 6, USA) brand only.

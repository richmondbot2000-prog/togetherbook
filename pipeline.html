Application pipeline

How customers who started a Transform Credit application in March 2026 are progressing through underwriting, as of today. Outcomes still develop — applicants from late in the month are mostly still in flight.

Two entry points converge: leads we purchased from affiliates (broker / PPC inventory bid in the Brokers API) and direct applications that came straight to the website without a paid lead.

Caveats: (a) the "paid out" stage counts loans currently in the Live-Loan state, so loans already settled / paid off drop out — the funnel understates total funded volume; (b) the "March cohort" is defined by InterestingDateTimeUtc, which is the application's most-recent status-update timestamp, not its creation date — a late-March application whose next status change was on 1 April moves out of the cohort.

A storybook of the Central Services platform

Eight helpers, twelve robots,
and one very tidy ledger.

TogetherBOOK explains how Richmond Group's lending platform actually works — in plain English, with characters you can remember by name. Onboarding, pairing on a bug, or simply curious: every page tells you what each piece does, who calls it, and what to ask if you need something from it.

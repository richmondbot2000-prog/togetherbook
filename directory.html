A list of everyone who works for us, with us, or who has access to one or more of the systems we work with. More

Where this list comes from. Each person here was added either by hand (using + Add person at the bottom of the page) or because they showed up in one of the systems we connect to — Google Workspace (Letme and Together Loans Gmail), our Warehouse CRM, our payroll system, or our Auth0 access list. We re-check those systems every hour, and anyone new appears under Unmatched accounts for you to match to an existing person or set up as a new one.

How to change someone's details. Tap a row to open that person's page. There you can edit their name, role, phone, address, start and end date, manager, and so on — click Edit on their info box, make your edits, then Save. For Google accounts (suspending, forwarding mail, deleting, adding an alias) open the Accounts tab on the same page. To add a brand-new person use + Add person at the bottom; to remove someone, do it from their own page.

Who's allowed to change what. TogetherBook admins can edit any field on any person, and run system-wide actions like Unmatched accounts and Sync access. Everyone else can just edit their own details.

Source-quality analysis

All sections below are derived from source-quality.json — a daily heavy-join analysis that aggregates leads by (Broker, SourceReference1) cell over a 60-day window ending 30 days ago (the 30-day lag gives paid-out outcomes time to settle). The canonical KPI from the Partnerships Handbook is blended CPA (cost / paid-loan revenue) — target ≤12% to remain profitable. Cells are coloured against that target: ≤12% on plan · 12–20% watch · >20% cut.

Blocked Sources to consider re-enablingBlocked Refs with Accepts →

(Broker, Source) cells where we currently reject leads (LeadResultTypeId = -1 — Source excluded). We identity-match each rejected lead against subsequent purchases via a different (Broker, Source) cell — by SSN, Phone+DOB, or Email+DOB — counting only matches landing strictly after the rejection and within 30 days. High bounceback-paid counts mean blocking this cell is forfeiting funded loans we'd otherwise have written.

Manual review zone — 10–20% apply rate

Per the Partnerships Handbook, the nightly AutoBlock Robot only fires at ≤10% apply rate. Cells with apply rates between 10% and 20% are still underperforming but slip through the robot's net — they need a human decision. This is the population the Bad Sub-Affiliates report on reporting.rgcore.com is designed to flag. Same volume gate (50+ accepts) as the autoblock thresholds. Open Bad Sub-Affiliates report →

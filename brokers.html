Sources to consider blocking

Sources we currently buy leads from where the effective cost per paid loan exceeds $600 — the threshold above which we materially overpay for a funded loan via this channel. Volume-gated to 200+ purchased leads. Window: 60 days ending 30 days ago — that lag lets purchased leads finish maturing so today's snapshot reflects real outcomes rather than under-counted recent buys. Sources with no recorded cost (CPF/rev-share campaigns that haven't billed in the window) are excluded since "$0 per paid loan" is not a blocking signal. Sorted by cost-per-paid-loan descending.

Blocked sources to consider unblocking

Sources we currently reject (LeadResultTypeId = -1 — Source excluded). For each one we identity-match its rejected leads against people we DID buy from other sources in the same window — by SSN, or by Phone+DOB, or by Email+DOB. The window is 60 days ending 30 days ago so paid-out outcomes have had time to settle. If lots of those rejected people came back via another source and paid out, the audience is decent and the block may be costing us loans.

Sources where we overpay

For every purchased lead bought at an upfront per-lead price (CPL or auction-bid models), we identity-match the same person against later purchases in the window (60 days ending 30 days ago — a lag that lets outcomes mature). When a cheaper version of the same person reappears via a different campaign, the first buy was an overpay — we could have waited. Sources are ranked by total overspend in the window. Only sources with at least one cheaper-later clone appear.
